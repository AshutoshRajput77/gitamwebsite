About Us

GITAM has been established by Pawan Ganga Educational Society, with a pivotal aim to take quality education to the door steps of far furlong rural and urban masses of the country. GITAM is an institute duly approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education) and Affiliated to Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU, Rohtak, Haryana) & Haryana State Board of Technical Education (HSBTE, Panchkula) . GITAM is also Recognized under Section 2(f) of UGC Act , 1956. GITAM has been treading the path of rapid development as per a neatly charted action plan since its inception in 2008.
